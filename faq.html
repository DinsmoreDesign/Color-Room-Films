FAQ

We know you have tons of questions - so here's a few answers to our most frequently asked questions.

What makes us different?

Since every wedding is unique so is every video. We don't just slap a preset on your footage and call it a day. We bring your footage to life by color correcting and grading your footage to give the your wedding that classic Hollywood look.

When do I get my film?

Normally we can have a full finished wedding trailer to you in about 2 weeks, sometimes less. If you have a deadline you want to meet please let us know ahead of time, we have never made a bride wait past a deadline for her film.

How do I get my wedding film?

We suggest delivery via USB key but we also have DVD and Blu Ray options. As of now online download is not available due to the size of the films. See plan details for pricing.

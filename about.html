Owner: Steve DiMaggio

Steve considers himself a filmmaker, not a wedding videographer. His goal is is to tell your story through motion pictures. Before becoming a filmmaker, Steve was a professional musician and studio owner who traveled the country to record and play music. From New Jersey to California, and everywhere in between, he captured these stories through motion picture.

After 10 years of recording and being on the road, his passion for recording music had him, his soon to be wife and dog, Meeka, getting ready for a move halfway across the country to work for the leading retailer and designer in customer recording studios. While working in the midwest, Steve received a formal training in videography, cinematography techniques and equipment that formed the technical basis for his style, all while he continued to film bands and events as a courtesy to friends.

After half a decade in the midwest, Steve and his wife, Jane, decided to start a family and head back to New Jersey (now with 3 dogs and first daughter, Carmella). After a bit of soul searching when returning, Steve took an opportunity to manage and create video content for a national music store.

Through his wife's photography business, his video work organically grew to what it is today. Because of this gradual maturation process, Steve has been able to hone his skills and really dig into what each bride wants. His deepest core value is to deliver a wedding film that is honest and far above any previous expectation by the couple. He is able to accomplish this by keeping his rates low, using Hollywood-grade equipment and never allowing himself to be completely satisfied.

Visual

The goal for us is to the create the most honest film of your event or service. We specialize in cinematic films and making everyone feel like a star.

Audio

For close to three decades, Steve has dedicated himself to recording, live audio and audio production. He takes special pride in delivering the cleanest audio possible.

Timing

Quick turnaround and meeting deadlines are our forte. We have never had a missed deadline and in most cases, deliver your film ahead of schedule.
